Cold Water

Description

Is there anything better than a nice cup of room-temperature water on a hot day?
Why yes there is, and it's appropriately named cold water!
If you haven't yet experienced the wonder that is cold water, you are in for a wild ride! Some believe it's just a fad for the young crowds, but I think it's a treat that will last throughout the ages.

Ingredients

  • Room-temperature water
  • Ice
  • Patience

Steps

  1. Carefully place room-temperature water on an even surface.
  2. Gently drop ice cubes into glass.
  3. Wait until condensation appears on the side of the glass.
     If you are in a dry climate, wait 5-10 minutes.
  4. Enjoy!
