Room-Temperature Water

Description

Is there anything better on a warm day than a nice cup of room-temperature water? Not that I currently know of at this exact moment. Though rare in the wilderness, cups of room-temperature water can be found in nearly every human settlement. If you follow our steps listed below, you too can be a master of room-temperature water!

Ingredients

  • Water (any temperature)
  • A glass

Steps

  1. Wait for water to reach room-temperature. This may take several minutes.
  2. Pour water into glass.
  3. Enjoy!
